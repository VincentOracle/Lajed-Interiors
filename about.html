About Lajed Interiors

Crafting Spaces with Purpose, Since Day One

For more than two decades we've paired global sourcing with local craftsmanship — designing homes, offices and hospitality spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

Who We Are

More Than Designers

Lajed Interiors is a Nairobi-based studio that believes great design begins with listening. We create refined, functional, and enduring spaces that reflect each client's unique vision, lifestyle, and needs. Whether it's a family home, a boutique hotel, or a corporate office, we bring the same level of care and precision to every project.

Guided by creativity, integrity, and a client-focused approach, we strive to enhance daily living through spaces that are both visually compelling and purposefully designed to stand the test of time.

Our Compass

Mission & Vision

Mission

To create refined, functional, and enduring spaces that reflect each client's unique vision, lifestyle, and needs. We are dedicated to delivering thoughtful design solutions that seamlessly integrate aesthetics, comfort, and practicality while upholding the highest standards of quality and precision.

Vision

To be the premier provider of luxury interior solutions, recognized for transforming spaces through flawless design and enduring quality. We aim to elevate daily living by seamlessly blending high-end aesthetics with practical innovation, ensuring that every space we touch stands the test of time.

Global Reach, Local Heart

Product Sourcing Experience

We bring over 20 years of experience sourcing furnishings, fixtures, finishes, construction materials and decorative items — both for our own hotels and retail outlets, and for our clients.

  • International procurement. Having imported all the furniture and fittings for our hotels in Zanzibar, Nairobi, USA and Europe, we've built deep experience navigating international importation regulations.
  • Own sourcing offices in India and China, each staffed by professionals whose experience in sourcing, container management and shipping spans over 15 years.
  • Local manufacturing in Kenya, where we retain a team of professional tailors, upholsterers, metal workers and installation engineers.
  • Deep experience commissioning bespoke items worldwide, working comfortably with both large factories and small-scale artisans alike.
