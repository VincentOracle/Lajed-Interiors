About Lajed Interiors

Crafting Spaces with Purpose

With over two decades of experience, we combine global sourcing, local craftsmanship, and a deep respect for each client’s vision.

Who We Are

More Than Designers

Lajed Interiors is a Nairobi-based studio that believes great design begins with listening. We create refined, functional, and enduring spaces that reflect each client’s unique vision, lifestyle, and needs. Whether it’s a family home, a boutique hotel, or a corporate office, we bring the same level of care and precision to every project.

Our approach blends high-end aesthetics with practical innovation, ensuring that every space we touch stands the test of time.

Our Compass

Mission & Vision

Mission: To create refined, functional, and enduring spaces that reflect each client’s unique vision, lifestyle, and needs. We are dedicated to delivering thoughtful design solutions that seamlessly integrate aesthetics, comfort, and practicality while upholding the highest standards of quality and precision.

Vision: To be the premier provider of luxury interior solutions, recognized for transforming spaces through flawless design and enduring quality. We aim to elevate daily living by seamlessly blending high-end aesthetics with practical innovation, ensuring that every space we touch stands the test of time.

Global Reach, Local Heart

Product Sourcing Experience

We bring over 20 years of experience in sourcing furnishings, fixtures, and decorative items from across the globe.

  • International procurement — compliance with import regulations across Zanzibar, Nairobi, USA, and Europe.
  • Own sourcing offices in India and China, with teams of professionals spanning 15+ years in container management and shipping.
  • Local manufacturing in Kenya: professional tailors, upholsterers, metal workers, and installation engineers.
  • Experience working with both large factories and small-scale artisans worldwide.
